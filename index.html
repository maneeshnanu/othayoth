About

Vadakarayude hridhabaagathu sthithicheyyunnathanu othayoth temple. Puwranikamayi kalagattathil evide brahmanmarude bumiyayirunnu, koottamayi othu(vedham) chollukayum padippikkukayum cheyithirunnu anganeyanu othayothu enna naamam vannthu ,pilkalathu bumiyude avakashm vadakarayile prassidhamaya vannapalan kandi kuruppan marude adheenathayilayi , evideyulla karanavar balusseri kottayil paradevathayude (veettakorumakan) nithya sandharsakanayirunnu , angane pathivu sandharshanam kazhinju tharavattil ethiya karanavar uchayikku oonu kazhikkan irunnu , pkshe vayilolam ethathe chorurulakal nilathu veenu....! Baya chakithanaya karnavar ottum amandhikkathe jolsyare sameepichu , avidunnu prasnavasal koottayil paradevatha kude undennum enikkoru sthanam thannittu mathi ninte bojanam ennum kandu . Dayvanjante nirdhesha prakaram othayothu bumiyil pattupurayodu koodiya kshethram panikazhichu purvadhikam bomgiyayi aradhichu poonnu .pinnidu booparishkarana niyamavum tharavadinte kshayavum karanam kshetham nokkan pattathe nasichu poyi. Kshethrathinte sthalavum anyadheenamayi . Nasonmukamaya kshethrathe bakthajanagalude nirlobamaya sahaya sahakaranathode innu kanunna kshethramakki matti ,evide paradevathaye koodathe " krishnan , ganapathi , ayyappan enne murthikalum anugraha chorinju kondirikkunnu. Eppol vadakarayude adhyathmikamyi ettavum uyarachayilulla mahakshethramanu. Evide bkthajangalude unnamanathinu vendi vedha padanam(kasyapa ) , upanishth padanam ( swamini shivanadha puri ), akshara sloka sadhassu ,sathreeya sangeetham ,bajanam ,geetha padanam enniva nadathunnu ,evide pradhana petta divasam thingal , vyazham , velli ,sani enne dinangalanu. Paradevatha _thingal krishnan _ vyazham ganapathi _ velli ayyappan _ sani

Temple Management

The temple administration is basically run from the collections received from the offerings of the devotees. The 15 member committee comprises of the people from different religions, caste and creed a feature no other temple can ever boast of. The importance attached to social values is so great and the people participation unique.

How To Reach

The temple is location in the Heart of Vadakara. It is well connected by Rail and Road

By Rail

The pilgrims can reach Vadakara by Rail and from there you can walk or can get an auto to the temple.

By Air

The nearest airport is Calicut(75 KM). Another airport is coming nearby (Kannur - 47 KM). From The airport you can get Bus Directly to Vadakara Bus Stand. From Bus stand you can either walk or can get an auto to the temple.

By Road

The temple is at a walkable distance from Vadakara Bus Stand. Its located Off Kanyakumari Panvel Highway.
